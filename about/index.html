About Solar DIY Project

Practical solar DIY project guides, planning calculators, wiring explainers, and safety-first checklists for small off-grid systems.

Solar DIY Project is an independent educational site for small solar planning. The goal is to help readers understand loads, batteries, panels, wiring, and tradeoffs before they spend money on parts.

The site is written for sheds, RVs, cabins, camera poles, portable backup systems, and other small projects. It does not replace licensed electrical advice, local code review, engineering sign-off, or manufacturer documentation.

Editorial standards

Pages are planned around usefulness first: clear titles, readable structure, original examples, and updates when information changes. See the editorial policy for the full review approach.
